About Our Client

This well-established organisation is known for providing an excellent level of service to their customers and have maintained an 'Excellent' Trustpilot rating as well as launching unique products in the banking sector.

Job description

  • Lead the Project Team; motivate and inspire the team to deliver.
  • End to end project management from planning through to mobilisation and delivery
  • Deliver timely projects within constraints of budget / quality / scope / timeline parameters directly and through a team.
  • Identify requirements of the stakeholders, scoping projects, producing detailed plans, providing management information, as defined by the key stakeholders, and reporting the status to senior stakeholders.
  • Proactively & dynamically managing project plans, calling out dependencies, resources and other risks and suggesting mitigation.
  • Ensuring project post-implementation reviews against forecast delivery benefits are delivered.
  • Influences program sponsors to make the appropriate decisions and modifications in response to external influences on the program.
  • Support mentoring of Project / Scrum team members, share industry expertise to upskill existing Change team members.

The Successful Applicant

  • Ability to Project Manage complex mid to large scale projects.
  • A proven track record of delivering in a range of complex IT Change, Regulatory and Business Change projects.
  • Excellent stakeholder management experience; including leadership and influencing skills.
